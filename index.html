❓ Help
What is Mac Weird Keys?

An app to find, learn about, and copy symbols you can't easily type on your Mac keyboard.

How do I copy a symbol?

Click any symbol in the grid — it gets copied to your clipboard automatically!

What is X-Ray Mode?

X-Ray Mode shows all hidden characters behind Option and Shift+Option key combos.

Is my data private?

Yes. Everything runs locally in your browser. No data is sent anywhere.

⌨ Modifier Keys

Mac has 4 modifier keys: Command, Option, Control, Shift. Option is the key to most hidden symbols!

⌨ Keyboard Layouts

AZERTY is the French layout. QWERTY is the English/US layout. Symbols are in different places!

🔒 Privacy

Local-first, privacy-first. All data stays in your browser. No tracking, no analytics.
